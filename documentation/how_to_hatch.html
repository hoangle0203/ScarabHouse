How to hatch Scarab egg(s)

Be eligible to enter feeding level

As mentioned in the introduction section, ScaraboGhoul game is split into two stages including Scarab stage and Ghoul stage (not developed yet). This section will talk through how to feed, hatch Scarab egg(s) and have fun while playing in the feeding level.

In the game item managing interface, before hitting Play button to feed Scarab egg(s), user must conform to a simple rule to be eligible to move to the feeding level. That rule requires that user has to choose one token in available list. Feeding Scarab egg(s)

Game at the Scarab stage is not a challenging one, so in the feeding level, just left click to grab the magic shovel on the right side, left click to scoop any piece of animal dung.

With the shovel having dung in it, user can left click to feed Scarab egg(s) or throw at animal for fun. User can also right click to drop back the dung in the shovel or put the shovel back to initial location. Back button is used to get back to game item managing interface, but being disabled if the shovel is in control.

Hatching Scarab egg(s)

There are four types of animal dung including deer dung, elk dung, fox dung, and wolf dung. The maximal number of dung pieces taken into account is 99. When all of four types of animal dung reach to 99, Scarab egg is eligible for being hatched. If Hatch button is clicked while any of those four types is not accumulated enough to 99, an error message will appear at the top left corner to show the problem.

When knowing Scarab egg is eligible for being hatched, user can click Hatch button to send an order wallet interface, then click Confirm button to pay for hatching process. After transaction, user needs to wait 30 seconds Scarab egg to turn over a mature Scarab. Token Id of Scarab egg will be removed from blockchain and being replaced with a new token Id of the mature Scarab just hatched from.

Whitelist implementation

The way a user is added to the White List of this game is decided the number of Scarab tokens (in both egg form or mature form) hold in the wallet. Specifically, the reward of whitelisted users receive is how much royalty they gain when minting new token for mature Scarab after hatching process which can afford a decent passive income for the creator of that new token.

Royalty of mature Scarab token depends on the number of tokens user hold in their wallet at the time of hatching process:

  • Holding 1 egg: 1% royalty for smart contract creator.
  • Holding 2 to 4 eggs: no royalty.
  • Holding 5 to 9 eggs: 2% royalty for the user who hatches the Scarab egg.
  • Holding 10 or more eggs: 5% royalty for the user who hatches the Scarab egg.
